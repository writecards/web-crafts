shengli wen

American Artist’s Black Gooey Universe is a clever play on words that explores how technology exploits Blackness. American Artist writes "Racial Slavery -- which generated Blackness as a site of permanent extraction, gratuitous physical violence, and social death -- provided the material and ideological basis for the United States. Unsurprisingly, Silicon Valley advances this constitutive anti-blackness through its technological products and processes.” “Mother of all Demos II” is extremely powerful in the way that it forces viewers to think about how the Black workforce has historically been the least represented minority group in Silicon Valley. When trying to research background on American Artist, I found out he doesn’t have much public information, and this is an intentional decision, which speaks directly to how Black lives have been forgotten across fields of art, design, tech, etc. I found his pieces very compelling. His work is truly brilliant.
