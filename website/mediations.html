MEDIATIONS - shengli wen

Mediations is a lexicon of vocabularies, artworks, and movements that respond to shared and diverging experiences among the asian queer/trans diaspora. It uses Notion API as a CMS and was created with ViteJS, Open Props, HTML, and CSS. It’s hosted with Netlify’s serverless function.

Background

The name stems from how histories of both Asian bodies and queer/trans bodies have been mediated, as these communities haven’t always had agency in how their stories are told. The site is evolving in tandem with a series of physical gatherings exploring themes such as queerness, diaspora, racial and cultural identities, lineage, and temporality. This project views the webpage as a form of collaborative time-based media, a space to gather thoughts and ideas, a space that allows for change and growth over time along with the futures we imagine.

Inspiration

Mindy Seu’s cyberfeminism index serves as a huge source of inspiration, as well as Xin Wang’s Asian futurism archive on Tumblr. Laurel Schwulst is also a major influence in terms of visual aesthetic.

Reflection

  • add night mode
  • improve responsiveness
  • add a front-end interface for user submissions (and think about how to moderate?)
